# Nexus Orb: Design Specification & AI Prompts

## 1. Design Philosophy
**Direction: The Hybrid Fusion (Sentient Core)**
Combining **High-Tech** (Image 1) and **Gentle Companion** (Image 2).
*   **The Look:** "Soft Tech". Think Apple design or EVE from Wall-E. High-tech but approachable.
*   **The Shape:** A perfectly smooth, matte sphere (Friendly).
*   **The Tech:** Instead of a messy wireframe or a cartoon face, use a **glowing core** or a **minimalist geometric pattern** to suggest intelligence.
*   **Keywords:** Ethereal, Clean, Sentient, Premium.

## 2. Visual Style Guide
*   **Primary Color:** Mint Green (`#6EE7B7`) shell with Deep Emerald (`#10B981`) core.
*   **Material:** Frosted Glass (translucent) or Soft-Touch Matte Plastic.
*   **Lighting:** Internal glow (Biotech feel).
