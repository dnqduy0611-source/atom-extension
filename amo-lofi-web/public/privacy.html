Amo Lofi — Cinematic Focus

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026  ·  Last Updated: February 22, 2026

TL;DR — Amo Lofi does not sell your data, does not track your browsing history, and does not serve ads. We only collect what's needed to make focus sessions work and sync across devices.

1. Introduction

Amo Lofi — Cinematic Focus ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that replaces your New Tab page with a cinematic focus environment featuring a Pomodoro timer, ambient scenes, and productivity tools.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from Google OAuth. This is used solely to identify your account for syncing preferences and focus sessions across devices.

2.2 Usage & Focus Session Data

We collect anonymous product analytics to improve the extension:

Data Purpose
Timer events (start, pause, complete) Pomodoro statistics & productivity insights
Scene selections Understanding popular scenes to improve content
Feature usage (e.g., site blocker toggles) Product improvement

2.3 Local Storage Data

The following data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never sent to our servers:

  • Timer settings (focus/break durations)
  • Selected scene and variant preferences
  • Blocked sites list
  • UI preferences (theme, language)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, access, or store any of the following:
  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Web page content
  • Passwords or financial information
  • Personal files or documents
  • Location data
  • Health information
  • Contact lists or messages

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Sync across devices: Your focus sessions and scene preferences sync between the extension and the AmoLofi web app when you're signed in.
  • Productivity insights: Focus session data powers your personal statistics dashboard and optional AI productivity reports.
  • Product improvement: Aggregated, anonymous usage data helps us understand which features are most valuable.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider for authentication and data storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements. No other third parties have access to your data.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication is handled via Google OAuth 2.0 (industry standard)
  • Database access is protected by Row-Level Security policies
  • We follow the principle of least privilege for data access

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Use without signing in: The extension works fully without an account. No data is sent to our servers if you don't sign in.
  • Delete your account: You can sign out at any time. Contact us to request complete data deletion.
  • Clear local data: You can clear all local extension data by removing and reinstalling the extension.

8. Chrome Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions, each for a specific purpose:

Permission Why We Need It
tabs Display the focus dashboard on new tabs and open the web app
storage Save preferences and settings locally
alarms Pomodoro timer phase transitions
notifications Alert when focus/break sessions end
identity Google Sign-In for cross-device sync
topSites Show frequently visited sites on the new tab page

9. Children's Privacy

Amo Lofi is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
